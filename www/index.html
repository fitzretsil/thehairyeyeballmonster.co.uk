The Hairy Eyeball Monster

                                                       

"The Hairy Eyeball Monster lives in a forest dark and deep, with lots of other creatures who fly and crawl and creep - to hide when he is close, but what is there to fear?

Come and learn the secrets of the monsters hiding here!"



About

Alice Hailwood wrote and drew this book for a school project several years ago and would now like to share it with a wider audience. She hopes you have as much fun reading it as she did in creating it.
